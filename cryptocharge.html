How it Works

1

Apply

Fill out a simple application form for one of our 4 types of debit cards. Choose one that best suits your needs.

2

Verify

Once your information has been verified, we will ship your card to the address of your choosing.

3

Activate

When your card arrives in the mail, activate your card at (website url).

4

Load

Load your card with Fiat currency (USD, Euro, Pounds) by converting any of the cryptocurrencies in your wallet.

5

Spend

Make purchases or cash withdrawals at ATMs around the world.
